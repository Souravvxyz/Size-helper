Holiday Size Finder

Use this tool to find your recommended clothing size. Simply select your height, weight, and body type below.

Our Holiday Size Finder uses your height and weight brackets along with your body type to recommend a clothing size (S, M, L, or XL). The data is based on standard size-guide information – for instance, someone in the 160-169 cm and 60-69 kg range will likely be a medium, but adjustments are made if you are slim or heavy.

This tool simplifies your online shopping by giving a general recommendation to reduce guesswork. It’s especially useful during the busy holiday season when coordinating matching family outfits is a fun way to celebrate.

Disclaimer: This tool provides a general recommendation based on preset brackets and limited input. It is not a substitute for professional fitting advice. Actual sizes may vary by brand and style.
